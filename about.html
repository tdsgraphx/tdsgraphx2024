Brand Strategy

A branding strategy defines who the company identifies with, what values it promotes, and how it approaches customers’ emotional connections. On the other hand, a branding campaign refers to specific branding tactics like branding colors, logos, slogans, line extensions, etc. values. The process starts with a well-defined strategy and develops through a series of branding campaigns.

Brand Identity

When creating brand identities we first come up or build a brand strategy to determine how the brand identity should be percieved or the way it is being currently perceived. This is how we create unique eye-catching brand identities sending a strong reliable business message to customers. So without a clear brand strategy, there's no brand identity.
